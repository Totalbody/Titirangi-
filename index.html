About Us

Total Body Physio Titirangi has been serving the community since 2004. Our clinic, located in the heart of Titirangi Village, offers expert physiotherapy care in a welcoming environment. Our team of experienced physiotherapists is dedicated to providing personalized treatment plans to help you achieve optimal health and well-being.

Whether you're recovering from an injury, managing chronic pain, or looking to improve your overall physical function, we're here to support you every step of the way. Our approach combines hands-on treatment with education and exercise prescription to ensure long-lasting results.

Frequently Asked Questions

What should I expect during my first visit?
During your first visit, our expert physiotherapist will conduct a comprehensive assessment. This includes discussing your medical history, current symptoms, and goals. We'll perform a physical examination to evaluate your condition. Based on this assessment, we'll develop a personalised treatment plan tailored to your specific needs and explain the proposed course of action.
Do I need a referral to see a physiotherapist?
In New Zealand, you generally don't need a referral to see a physiotherapist for private treatment. You can book an appointment directly with us. However, if you're seeking treatment for an ACC-covered injury, you may need a referral from your GP or specialist. If you're unsure, give us a call, and we'll be happy to guide you through the process.
What should I wear to my appointment?
We recommend wearing comfortable, loose-fitting clothing that allows easy access to the area being treated. For lower body issues, shorts or loose trousers are ideal. For upper body concerns, a tank top or loose shirt works well. If you're coming directly from work, don't worry - we can provide gowns or shorts if needed.
