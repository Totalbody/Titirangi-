Frequently Asked Questions

What is an ACC injury?
An ACC injury refers to an injury covered by the Accident Compensation Corporation in New Zealand. This includes injuries from accidents at work, home, or during sports activities. ACC provides support for treatment and rehabilitation of these injuries.
How do I book an appointment?
You can book an appointment online through our website or by calling us at (09) 817 7377.
What are your prices?
For ACC-covered injuries, the initial consultation is $35, and follow-up sessions are $30. For private patients, the initial consultation is $90, and follow-ups are $80.
