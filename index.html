About Our Clinic

Total Body Physio Titirangi has been proudly serving the community since 2004. Our clinic, conveniently located in the heart of Titirangi Village, offers expert physiotherapy care in a welcoming environment.

Our team of experienced physiotherapists is dedicated to providing personalized treatment plans to help you achieve optimal health and well-being. Whether you're recovering from an injury, managing chronic pain, or looking to improve your overall physical function, we're here to support you every step of the way.
